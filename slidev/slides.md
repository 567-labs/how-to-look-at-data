# Key Takeaways

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-8">

<div class="p-6 bg-gray-50 rounded-lg">

## Measure Inputs
- Public benchmarks ≠ Your data
- Fast evals make decisions easy
- Focus on retrieval accuracy
- Test on YOUR corpus

<div class="p-6 bg-gray-50 rounded-lg">
